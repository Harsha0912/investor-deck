01

Casinos Don't Know What
Their Customers Are Worth

Every comp decision — free rooms, dinners, show tickets — comes from one formula:

Average Bet × Hands/Hour × House Edge × Time

The most important variable — house edge — is wrong for 98% of players. The system assumes everyone plays optimally, but fewer than 2% actually do.

"Of the regular players that come to my table, less than 2% play perfect strategy all the time." — Veteran blackjack dealer, Casino Center
